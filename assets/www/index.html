How to Play:

BlackJack is a simple casino card game, in which player plays to get his score 21 or near to it.
In BlackJack, one has to make his score 21 or near to it and also he has to score more than Dealer/CPU. Scoring 21 is BlackJack, you win and scoring more than 21 is burst, you lose.
Here, scoring is done on values of cards.Each face card(Joker,Queen or King) has value 10. An Ace card(A) may have value 1 or 10.Other cards have the same value of their numbers.
Some terms in BlackJack:
1. Hit - Draw a card from deck.
2. Stay - Stop the round.
You can also bet if you want.
